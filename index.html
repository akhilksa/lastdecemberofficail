Bio

Formed in 2014 Last December is a rock duo from India, consisting of Emil William(Lead Vocals, Guitar) & Gidel Jacob (Bass,Backing Vocals).

Showing an interest in music at a very young age, they started playing instruments making songs. Inspired by bands such as Green Day, Linkin Park, Dream Theater, Coldplay, their music goes everywhere from pop to hard rock.

Currently working on their first EP, the band collaborated with a Russian band, Forget my silence, releasing their first single 'With no tears'.
